💡 How to use this board

  1. Enter your name in the field at the top left — you must do this before posting or replying.
  2. Click "✨ Post a Question" to add a new question to the board. Keep it clear and specific so classmates can help!
  3. Reply to a classmate by typing in the reply box at the bottom of any note and clicking Reply.
  4. Mark a question as answered by clicking Mark answered on any note — the card will turn green so everyone knows it's resolved.
  5. Need urgent help? Click Email instructor on any note to open a pre-filled email to your instructor. Fill in your name and describe your question or concern, then hit send.
  6. Filter the board using the buttons on the right to view All, Open, or Answered questions.
  7. New posts? A pink banner will appear at the top when classmates have added new questions — click it to refresh the board.
